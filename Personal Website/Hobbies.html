"Art speaks where words are unable to explain." - Various

Perhaps it all started with Lego... As a kid, I could spend hours simply building sets, taking them apart, and making something new.
Then, I progressed on to machinery. Seeing the things engineers can create is really inspiring. Ships and spacecraft were among my favourites.
My most recent artistic endeavour is graphic design. I enjoy creating posters and art based on posters I wish existed, but do not.

"Life's too short to drive boring cars!" - Various

If I had to keep only one of my hobbies, this would definitely be it.
My love for cars stretches back over a decade, and shows no signs of stopping.
Almost every one of my other hobbies revolve around the automotive; I tried my hand at photography in order to take better pictures of cars.
I learned graphic design to create art prints and car posters. Even my musical taste is shaped by the car community - I like Eurobeat as a genre, not just a meme.
My gaming addiction started with the Need for Speed franchise, and I would love to eventually develop a proprietary tuning program with the knowledge I learn from this college program.
Ultimately, I see cars as a form of art; more than just an object, but an expression of one's self. More time should be spent enjoying the car than money.
That isn't to say my car isn't my most expensive investment - and continues to rise in cost as I add more cool stuff to it - but I believe any good car can be fun.
