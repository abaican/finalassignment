STATUS POST 12/11/2021

Following up on the last post... have you ever wondered what things will be like in a year?
It's such a useless thing to think about! You can never really know, so why even wonder?
Though, stranger still, is thinking that, maybe, you from one year in the future may remember this exact day.
Could you then say that the future you is spying on the current you?
I've thought about that once while driving home one day. I remembered an exact moment that happened a few weeks prior.
Back then, I had no idea that I would think back to then.
In that moment, I simply stopped and said "Hi!" to my future self.

And here I am now, waving right back...

STATUS POST 12/10/2021

Do you ever stop to remember exactly what happened a year ago from today?
I find it really interesting sometimes to go through old photos and find whatever pictures, videos, or screenshots I took on that particular date in time.
Sometimes, I like what I find. Other times, not so much...
But that doesn't mean it's not fun!
It's good to remember things you enjoyed. And if you don't like what you see, that's a reminder that you're a better you today!
And I find that comforting, in a way.
Indeed; we are not the same person from day to day. How could we expect to be the same we were one year ago?

That's my thought for today. I'll post something else tomorrow (if I remember!)
